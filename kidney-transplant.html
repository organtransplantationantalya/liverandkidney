About the Procedure

When Is a Kidney Transplant Needed?

Kidney transplantation is the best treatment option for patients with end-stage chronic kidney disease. It eliminates the need for dialysis and restores a full life.

  • Chronic kidney disease (CKD)
  • Diabetic nephropathy
  • Glomerulonephritis
  • Polycystic kidney disease
  • Hypertensive nephrosclerosis

Types of Transplant

Living Donor & Cadaveric

Living Donor

A kidney from a compatible living donor (relative). Best long-term outcomes and the ability to schedule surgery at the optimal time.

Cadaveric Transplant

Placement on the national waiting list. We ensure emergency readiness when a compatible organ becomes available.
